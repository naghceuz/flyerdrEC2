What is FlyerDR?

FlyerDR is a platform that help you manage your flight miles.

How to use FlyerDR

You link FlyerDR to your Airline Membership by providing the necessary information of your airline account. You can link FlyerDR with multiply airline accounts as long as these airline membership is all under your name. link it now?

What can FlyerDR do?

After you link your airline Membership with FlyerDR. You can check the status of your different airline membership all in one page. FlyerDR will tell you what is the current balance of your flight credit and which portion of the credit will be experied of what date. Even better, FlyerDR will enable you to enter the flight credit into your account if you forgot to show your membership to the airline staff. Also, on the section of Strategy section, FlyerDR will teach you the best strategy to manage your flight credit.
